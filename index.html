Quota Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 7 July 2026

Information We Collect

The bot stores only the information necessary for its operation.

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Quota and activity statistics
  • Server configuration settings

The bot does not collect email addresses, passwords, payment information, or any data outside of Discord.

Direct Messages

The bot will send direct messages to users to alert them to quota updates, strikes ect.

How Data Is Used

  • Track quotas and progress
  • Provide reminders and alerts
  • Maintain bot features
  • Improve reliability and performance

Data Sharing

No collected information is sold, rented, or shared with third parties unless required by law.

Data Retention

Data is stored only for as long as required to provide the bot's services and may be removed upon request where appropriate.

Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot after updates have been published constitutes acceptance of those changes.

GDPR Compliance

As members of the general public, you are entitled to the 'Right to access' (Be informed of what data is against your person) and the 'Right to be forgotten'. (Have all data tied to you deleted) If you so wish to excersise these rights, you are to send an email to the address linked below, and request it from there.
